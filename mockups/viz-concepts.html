MAVSim Viewer - Visualization Concepts

All derived from HIL_STATE_QUATERNION (position, orientation, velocity, airspeed, time)

Position / Velocity Derived

Velocity Vector Arrow

3D arrow from drone showing actual travel direction. The angle between nose (white) and velocity (green) reveals sideslip and drift — critical for controller tuning.

vx, vy, vz + quaternion

Ground Track Projection

Shadow/line directly below the drone on the grid plane. Makes altitude separation instantly readable without checking the HUD numbers.

position (x, y, z)

Distance / Altitude Ruler

Thin vertical line from drone to ground with tick marks every N meters. A visual altimeter that works from any camera angle.

position.y (altitude)

Speed Ribbon Trail

Trail thickness scales with ground speed. Instantly shows acceleration/deceleration zones. Thin = slow, wide = fast.

ground_speed (from vx, vy)
Quaternion / Attitude Derived

Attitude Ghost Horizon

A small artificial horizon disc near the drone showing bank and pitch. Like a cockpit instrument floating in 3D space — readable at any zoom level.

quaternion (roll, pitch)

Body Axis Indicators

Short colored lines: red=right, green=up, blue=forward. Shows orientation at a distance where the model shape is ambiguous (especially for symmetric quads).

quaternion

Angle of Attack Visualization

The angle between velocity vector and nose direction. For fixed-wing and VTOL, shows stall risk. Arc fills red as AoA increases toward critical angle.

quaternion + velocity
Time Series / Trail Analysis

G-Force Heatmap Trail

Trail colored by instantaneous G-load computed from velocity deltas between samples. Blue=1G (straight flight), yellow=2G, red=3G+ (hard turns).

d(velocity) / dt from trail buffer

Oscillation Detector

When roll or pitch rapidly alternates sign, that's a PID tuning problem. Trail pulses red/orange at oscillation zones with a warning indicator.

trail_roll[], trail_pitch[] sign changes

Loiter Circle Detection

Detects when trail forms a repeating circle and draws a fitted guide ring with radius label. Shows how well the controller maintains the commanded loiter radius.

position history (circular regression)
HUD Additions

Flight Time Counter

Elapsed sim time from time_usec. Shows total flight duration, arm time, and phase splits (takeoff, cruise, landing).

time_usec

Max Altitude / Speed Markers

Small flag markers on the trail at peak values — highest point, fastest speed, hardest turn. Quick visual reference for flight envelope.

max(altitude, speed, g-force) from trail

Top-Down Minimap

2D inset showing the trail from above with North indicator. Useful when the 3D camera is low-angle and the flight path is hard to read spatially.

position.x, position.z (top-down)
